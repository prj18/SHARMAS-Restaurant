About Us

History

Sharma's was founded in 1980 by
Mr. Pranjal Sharma in the heart of Delhi, India. From humble beginnings as a small family-run eatery, Sharma's has grown into an international chain of restaurants known for its authentic Indian cuisine. Our dedication to quality and tradition has won us loyal customers around the world.

Branches

Today, Sharma's boasts branches in several countries, including the USA, UK, Canada, Australia, and the UAE. Each branch offers a unique dining experience, maintaining the high standards of flavor and hospitality that our customers have come to expect.

Mission

Our mission is to bring the taste of India to the world, offering a diverse menu that caters to all palates. From spicy curries to sweet desserts, every dish is a testament to our commitment to culinary excellence.
